Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant city of Juba, Luxe Juba offers an unparalleled fine dining experience that celebrates both international cuisine and authentic South Sudanese flavors.

Our master chefs craft exquisite dishes using the freshest local ingredients alongside imported specialties, creating a culinary journey that delights the senses.

With elegant ambiance, impeccable service, and attention to every detail, we invite you to indulge in an extraordinary dining experience that honors the rich cultural heritage of South Sudan while embracing global culinary excellence.

Meet Our Chef

Michael Adebayo

Executive Chef

With over 15 years of experience in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe and Africa, Chef Michael brings his unique fusion of African and French culinary techniques to Luxe Juba.

Sarah Johnson

Head Pastry Chef

Trained in Paris and specializing in African-inspired desserts, Chef Sarah creates exquisite sweet endings to your dining experience that are both visually stunning and delicious.

James Okafor

Sous Chef

Born and raised in South Sudan, Chef James brings authentic local flavors and traditional cooking methods to our kitchen, creating a true taste of South Sudan with a modern twist.

Our Story

Building exceptional homes with passion, precision, and unparalleled craftsmanship since 1998.

Building Dreams Since 1998

Haven Homes was founded with a singular vision: to create extraordinary living spaces that reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of our clients while setting new standards for luxury, craftsmanship, and sustainability.

Our journey began when founder Michael Anderson, an architect with a passion for timeless design, recognized the need for a builder who could seamlessly blend architectural excellence with uncompromising construction quality. What started as a small boutique firm has grown into one of the most respected luxury home builders in the region.

Over the past two decades, we've had the privilege of creating more than 150 custom homes and completing countless renovation projects, each one a testament to our commitment to excellence and our clients' vision.

Our Philosophy

Uncompromising Quality

We believe that true luxury lies in the details. From foundation to finish, every element of your home is crafted with precision and care using the finest materials available.

Collaborative Approach

Your vision is our blueprint. We work closely with you throughout the entire process, ensuring your home reflects your personality, lifestyle, and aspirations.

Sustainable Excellence

We integrate environmentally responsible practices and materials without compromising on luxury, creating homes that are both beautiful and sustainable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Custom home projects typically take 12-18 months from design to completion, while renovations generally require 6-12 months depending on scope and complexity.

Do you work with client architects?

Yes, we frequently collaborate with client-hired architects. Our team works closely with them to ensure design feasibility and construction excellence.

What geographic areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Southern California including Los Angeles, Orange County, Santa Barbara, and surrounding areas. We consider select projects outside this region.

What is your design process?

Our process begins with discovery, followed by conceptual design, detailed development, construction documentation, and finally construction administration.
